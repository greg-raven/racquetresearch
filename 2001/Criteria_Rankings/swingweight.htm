Swingweight Rankings - July 2001

Published swingweight specs are based on the axis 10 cm from the butt, so these figures were converted using the Parallel Axis Theorem into the swingweight for the 7 cm axis, where it would be for a forehand. Low swingweight is good for "maneuverability" but bad for crushing through the ball on impact.
