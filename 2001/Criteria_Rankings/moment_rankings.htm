Moment Rankings 176 Racquets - July 2001

Low Moment is good because it makes the racquet head easier to hold up. Moment is the force twisting the head down, and is measured at the hand axis (7 cm from the butt end). It is the product of the weight (mass in kg*9.81) and the distance from the hand to the balance point in meters. For kids and weaker players, this should be an important criterion.
