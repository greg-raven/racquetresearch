Elbow Crunch 2001

Elbow Crunch should be low, to minimize the force compressing the elbow upon impact, when the centripetal force about that joint suddenly becomes more than the centrifugal force and produces what is effectively a muscle spasm that could tear the elbow tissues. The Elbow Crunch rankings under the five benchmark conditions were weighted, then these weighted ranks were summed to produced the weighted sum that determined the overall rank.
