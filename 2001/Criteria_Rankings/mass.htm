Mass Rankings - July 2001

Mass in kilograms, multiplied by the gravitational constant on the earth (9.81), gives the gravitational force, in Newtons, which is the weight of the racquet. In the English system, pounds are used instead of Newtons. Low mass means the racquet feels lighter, which feeble players think is important. Expert players, however, are well aware of the necessity of bringing plenty of mass to the collision with a fast moving ball, so for them these rankings should be reversed.
