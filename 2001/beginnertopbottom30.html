Top/Bottom 30 for Beginners- July 2001

Based on racquet measurements done by technicians at the U.S. Racquet Stringers Association, published in Racquet Tech. These models are currently being shipped by the manufacturers. To see the specs of discontinued models, click here.

The rankings under the macro criteria were weighted thus: 1.5*Elbow Safety + 0.5*Dexterity + Wrist Safety + Shoulder Safety + Efficiency. To see the rankings under the macro criteria, click on the blue letters. To see the rankings on racquets for experts, click here. To see the worst 30 racquets for beginners, click here.
