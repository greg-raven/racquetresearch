Mass Rankings - 2002

Mass in kilograms, multiplied by the gravitational acceleration (9.81 meters per second per second), gives the weight of the racquet, a force acting at its mass center (balance point). In the English system, pounds are used instead of Newtons. Low mass means the racquet feels lighter, which feeble players think is important. Expert players, however, are well aware of the necessity of bringing plenty of mass to the collision with a fast moving ball, so for them these rankings should be reversed.
