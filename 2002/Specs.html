Specifications of 167 Racquets — 2002

The U.S. Racquet Stringers Association (USRSA) has compiled this database of racquet specifications for models that are currently being shipped by their manufacturers. For the specs of discontinued models, click here. When plugged into the racquet evaluation formulas, these specs, together with the constants supplied by the five benchmark conditions, resulted in values under the evaluation criteria, which values were then sorted and the racquets ranked accordingly.
