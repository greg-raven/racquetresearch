Racquets for Expert Players 2002

The following overall rankings are based on the official database of racquet measurements done by technicians at the U.S. Racquet Stringers Association (http://www.racquettech.com/), published in their magazine Racquet Tech. To see the specs of discontinued models, click here.

The rankings under the macro criteria were weighted thus: 1.5*Elbow Safety + Wrist Safety + Shoulder Safety + Efficiency. Rankings are from best (Prince Graphite Classic OS) to worst (Wilson Triad Hammer 5.0 MP). The difference between these rankings and those for weak players is that the macrocriterion of Dexterity is not included here. If you are intermediate strength and above, you should use these rankings.
